#kyle_for_ukraine
Resurrection of Ukraine
(for our Slavic cousins)
The kind of faith forged in the same dark nights that make the beloved Madonnas black.
Pressure that makes diamonds from simple stone beads.
History that cycles through decades and eras.
Poland, and her people, on the Cross.
My adopted babcia,
A woman with resolute faith, though born during an atheist era.
A woman of great conviction,
Despite not knowing if she, her fellow Poles, or the diaspora, would live
To see Barwicki’s Resurrection of Poland reanimate history.
Feast of Assumption 2021,
10,000 Poles and Polonia pray together,
On our knees at the shrine.
Many kneel before Our Lady of Fatima,
Begging for the conversion of the heart of Russia,
Mother of God, pray for us sinners...
We should have prayed harder.
This summer we will again pilgrimage to the shrine.
Together, fervently praying the Rosary,
For the heart of Russia,
And the resurrection of Ukraine.
